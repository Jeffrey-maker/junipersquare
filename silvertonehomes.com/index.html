Meet Marc Silver

With over 25 years of life experience in Philadelphia, Marc Silver is the go-to real estate agent for downtown neighborhoods. His intimate knowledge of the real estate market, combined with his passion for architecture and the city’s culture, make him the ideal guide to buy or sell a home.

His skills in negotiation, market analysis, and client service have resulted in record sales and satisfied buyers and sellers. With a lifelong passion for real estate and helping others, Marc provides a tailored experience for each client's needs.
